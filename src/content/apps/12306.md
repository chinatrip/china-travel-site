# 12306 — China Railway Booking Platform

12306 is the official ticketing platform for China Railway and the only authoritative channel for high-speed, bullet, and conventional train tickets.

## Basics

- **Website**: https://www.12306.cn (English version available)
- **Hotline**: 12306 (English support available)
- **App**: App Store / Google Play; English UI
- **Booking window:** Tickets go on sale **15 days before departure**. Book as soon as the window opens for popular routes and holiday periods — popular G-train seats sell out quickly.

## For Foreign Users

- Registration requires a phone number (foreign numbers accepted) and a passport number.
- Tickets must be booked under your real passport details.
- E-tickets: scan the QR code at the turnstile — carry your **original passport** as the gate may require it for verification.
- Counter pickup: passport required.

## Payment Options

- **Alipay, WeChat Pay** (recommended for foreign visitors — reliably accepted)
- UnionPay debit / credit
- Visa / Mastercard at the counter (acceptance varies; use Alipay/WeChat Pay as primary)
- RMB cash (counter only)

## Seat Classes and Train Types

| Code | Type | Key seat classes |
|---|---|---|
| G | High-speed (350 km/h) | Second class, First class, Business |
| D | Bullet / intercity (250 km/h) | Second class, First class |
| Z/T/K | Conventional overnight | Seat, Hard sleeper (6-berth), Soft sleeper (4-berth) |

## Reissue and Refund Rules

Reissue (换乘): allowed before departure via app / website / counter; normally one reissue per ticket.

Refund fees for **G/D high-speed trains** (per 12306 official refund policy):

| Time before departure | Refund fee |
|---|---|
| ≥ 48 hours | 0% (full refund) |
| 24 – 48 hours | 5% |
| 2 – 24 hours | 10% |
| 15 min – 2 hours | 20% |
| < 15 minutes, or after departure | No refund |

Regular-speed K/T/Z trains follow different rules — verify at 12306.cn for your specific train type.

## Alternatives for Foreign Users

**Trip.com** and **Klook** offer English-language 12306 booking with customer support — useful if 12306's registration or payment step proves problematic. Both charge a service fee on top of the face-value ticket price. Book directly on 12306 to avoid this markup; the 12306 app and website have English support and are the recommended path for most visitors. (Source: Reddit r/travelchina, 2026-05-08)

## Service Highlights

- Nationwide station coverage with real-time seat availability
- E-tickets (QR scan at gate) and paper tickets supported
- Major stations (Grade 1 / 2 hub stations) offer staffed baggage storage and lost-and-found
- English announcements and signage at major hubs
